## Quick

In event preview page mode, the content displayed in the bottom explorer is customizable.

### Create categories

Right mouse button preset categories can be created quickly.

![](img/Optional/007/001.png)

The content displayed can be pictures, standing avatar videos, interfaces, Command, etc. Here is an example of standing avatar.

![](img/Optional/007/002.png=600-)

The 1-default standing avatar chosen here is based on the classification of the standing avatar module.

![](img/Optional/007/003.png)

Just click OK at the end.

![](img/Optional/007/004.png=600-)

If you need to modify the order of the categories, you can right click the mouse to modify it, or you can drag it directly.

![](img/Optional/007/005.gif)
