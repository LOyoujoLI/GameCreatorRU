## Importing material
 Click to add a new avatar（[How to import my resources](?file=003-GC本体文档/0008-资源/002-导入资源)）。<br>
 <img src="img/2/行走图/1-7.png" /><br>
 Select one or more avatars and click OK. When selecting one, it can be a single map or an omnidirectional avatar.<br>
 <img src="img/2/行走图/1-9.png" /><br>
 Once the import is complete, there are corresponding frame maps for each of the four directions, all with 4 frames each.<br>
 <img src="img/2/行走图/1-17.png" height=200 />
 <img src="img/2/行走图/1-18.png" height=200 />
 <img src="img/2/行走图/1-19.png" height=200 />
 <img src="img/2/行走图/1-20.png" height=200 /><br>
 The unchecked all-direction avatar means that only the currently selected direction is imported, and there is only one frame map, so the horizontal and vertical cut is 1.<br>

### Directional mode
You can also set the orientation mode when importing resources to indicate which direction the avatar material is<br> 
(if it differs from the GC pre-made orientation mapping then you will need to process the image material)

 <table>
   <tr>
     <td><b>Directional mode</b></td>  <td><b>Description</b></td>
   </tr>
    <tr>
    <td>1 Direction</td>
    <td>Only the left (4) direction of the avatar, switching to the right (6) direction will flip horizontally<br>(Tip: the actual material can not necessarily face the left direction, depending on the needs of the game.) <br>==Not automatically flipped when locked==</td>
    </tr>
    <tr> <td>2 directions</td><td>Only the left (4) and right (6) directions of the avatar</td> </tr>
    <tr> <td>3 directions</td><td>Only the left (4) and up (8) and down (2) directions of the avatar,<br> switching to the right (6) direction will show the left direction of the horizontally flipped map</td></tr>
    <tr> <td>4-directional</td><td>Left (4), right (6), up (8), and down (2) directions of avatar</td></tr>
    <tr> <td>5-directional</td><td>Left (4), upper (8), lower (2), lower left (1), upper left (7) directions of the avatar<br>Where right (6), lower right (3), upper right (9) will correspond to left (4), lower left (1), upper left (7) horizontal flip</td></tr>
    <tr> <td>8-directional</td><td>Has left (4), right (6), up (8), down (2), left down (1), left up (7), right down (3), and right up (9) directional avatar</td></tr>
 </table>

## Auxiliary Body
The auxiliary body is used for auxiliary calculations of the game, such as displaying the position of the animation.<br>
==Soon the same auxiliary body will be supported in different directions, movements, and frames with different parameters, so that it can be used to assist in calculating precise movements for games such as combat.==<br>
==Multiple auxiliaries will be added soon: circles, triangles, polylines, and line segments.==
