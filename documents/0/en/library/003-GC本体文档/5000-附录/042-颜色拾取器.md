# Appendix V: Color Picker
<img src="img/2/附录/2.png" /><br>
Colors can be selected by left-clicking in the color area. <br> 
Colors can be set directly by entering R/G/B or hexadecimal color values. <br> 
The color palette allows custom storage of frequently used colors:<br> 
-- Left mouse click on a preset color to quickly switch to that color. <br> 
-- Right mouse click on a blank area of the color palette to add it. <br> 
-- Right mouse click on an existing color to replace, delete, or move that preset color. <br>
