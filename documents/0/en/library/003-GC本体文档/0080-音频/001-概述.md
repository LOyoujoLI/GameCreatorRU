# Concept: Audio Video
Supports playback of audio and video, while audio also supports playback at different pitches.<br>
<table>
 <tr>
    <td>BGM-Background Music</td>  <td>Only one background music can be played at the same time, independent channel and total volume of background music can be controlled</td>
 </tr>
 <tr>
    <td>BGS-Ambient Sound</td>
    <td>
      Only one ambient sound effect can be played at the same time, with separate channels and control over the total volume of the ambient sound effect
    </td>
 </tr>
 <tr>
    <td>SE-Sound effects</td>
    <td>

    </td>
 </tr>
 <tr>
    <td>TS-Voice</td>
    <td>

    </td>
 </tr>
 <tr>

    </td>
 </tr>
 </table>

## Global volume control
 In addition to the volume itself set when playing audio, the global volume can also be modified and take effect in real time. <br> 
 The actual volume of an audio file is equal to its corresponding global volume level x the current audio volume level. <br> 
 For example, if the global volume is 50% and the current volume is 50%, the actual playback volume is 25%. <br>
