# Code example: Audio Video
BGM-Background music, only one background music can be played at the same time.<br>
BGS-Ambient sound effects, only one ambient sound effect can be played at the same time.<br>
SE-Sound effects that can be played simultaneously in an infinite stack, and if bound to a scene object, the object's proximity to the camera affects the sound size.<br>
[Concept: Audio](?file=003-GC本体文档/0080-音频/001-概述)<br>

### Play background music
>GameAudio.playBGM("xxx.mp3");

### Play background music with fade-in effect and 150% pitch
>//The last parameter 1.5 means 150% pitch, the middle true and 1000 means 1000 milliseconds fade in<br>
>GameAudio.playBGM("xxx.mp3",9999,true,1000,1.5);

### Play ambient sound effects
>GameAudio.playBGS("xxx.mp3");

### Playback sound effects
>GameAudio.playSE("xxx.mp3");

### Play a sound effect and bind it to the scene object so that the farther the camera is from the scene object, the quieter it will be
>// Get scene object #3 of the current scene (an instance of ClientSceneObject or its subclass)<br>
>var soc = Game.currentScene.sceneObjects[3] as ClientSceneObject;<br>
>GameAudio.playSE("xxx.mp3",1,1,soc);<br>

### Modify global sound volume
>// The volume of the global sound effect is set to 50% volume<br>
GameAduio.seVolume = 0.5;
