## Customized Conditional Divergence
In addition to the above categories of conditional divergence given by the system, custom conditional divergence is also supported. <br> 
Different items (templates) may have different custom conditional divergences. <br>
For more details, refer to [Customized Conditional Divergence](?file=003-GC本体文档/1010-自定义编辑器/040-自定义条件)
