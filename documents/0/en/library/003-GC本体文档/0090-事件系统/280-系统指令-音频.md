# Audio
Use this class of events to play [audio](?file=003-GC本体文档/0080-音频/001-概述) and stop [audio](?file=003-GC本体文档/0080-音频/001-概述).

## Events
<table>
 <tr>
    <td><b>Events</b></td> <td><b>Description</b></td>
 </tr>
 <tr>
    <td>Playing BGM</td> <td>Looping background music, only one background music is allowed to exist at the same time globally, playing new background music will replace the old<br>Fade in effect: the volume gradually increases from small to large within the specified time</td>
 </tr>
 <tr>
    <td>Play BGS</td> <td>
    Loop ambient sound, only one ambient sound is allowed to exist at the same time globally, playing a new ambient sound will replace the old<br> Fade in effect: the volume gradually increases from small to large over a specified period of time</td> 
    </td>
 </tr>
 <tr>
    <td>Playing SE</td> <td>Playing a single sound effect. Can be stacked for playback</td>
 </tr>
 <tr>
    <td>Playing voice</td> <td>Playing voice once, stackable</td>
 </tr>


 <tr>
    <td>Stop BGM</td> <td>Stop the background music being played<br>Fade out effect: the volume gradually decreases from loud to low for a specified period of time</td>
 </tr>
 <tr>
    <td>Stop BGS</td> <td>
    Stop playing ambient sound<br> Fade out effect: volume gradually decreases from high to low for a specified period of time</td>
     </td>
 </tr>
 <tr>
    <td>Stop SE</td> <td>Stop all currently playing sound effects</td>
 </tr>
 <tr>
    <td>Stop voice</td> <td>Stop all voices currently playing</td>
 </tr>
 </table>
