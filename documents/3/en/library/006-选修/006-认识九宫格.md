## Start

During the creation of the interface, the nine-box grid can assist in keeping the material from being stretched.

![](img/Optional/006/001.png)

Find the nine-pane grid in the Properties panel.

![](img/Optional/006/002.png)

Just make sure that the areas you don't want stretched are excluded from the yellow line, and since everyone's material is different, please adjust it to suit.

![](img/Optional/006/003.png)
