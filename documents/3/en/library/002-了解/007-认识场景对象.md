## Start

Scene objects are players, NPCs that can perform events in the game, and can even be decorative items such as doors with open animations and treasure chests.

![](img/Understand/007/001.jpg=500-)![](img/Understand/007/002.gif=450-)![](img/Understand/007/003.gif=500-)

## Attributes

When a scene object is selected, a variety of properties appear on the right side, and you can adjust the style of the scene object and the logic of its behavior through the displayed properties panel.

![](img/Understand/007/004.png=600-)

## Triggers

Scene objects have a variety of triggers to achieve different events by different trigger conditions

![](img/Understand/005/002.png=600-)

<table>
<tr><td>Click event</td><td>Event page executed when left mouse button or space is pressed against the scene object</td></tr>
<tr><td>Touch events</td><td>Event page executed when a player object touches the current scene object</td></tr>
<tr><td>Parallel events</td><td>Event pages that keep executing in a loop</td></tr>
<tr><td>Appear events</td><td>Event pages that execute when a scene object appears</td></tr>
<tr><td>Leave touch</td><td>Event page executed when a player object leaves the current scene object after touching it</td></tr>
</table>
