## Start

Conditional divergence is similar to the **Show Options** command we learned earlier, in that it also executes different commands by different situations.

For example, a dialogue is executed when the player has a certain item, and another dialogue is executed when he does not have a certain item.

![](img/Basic/004/001.png)

### Judgment method

Conditional divergence is executed by satisfying a judgment condition and not satisfying a judgment condition.

By default the condition divergence is only judged by satisfying the condition, and only when **Setting when condition is not satisfied** is checked will the judgment of not satisfying the condition appear.

![](img/Basic/004/002.png)
