## Obstacles

By adding obstacles, you can make it impossible for scene objects to walk past.

### Draw directly to the map

Simply draw directly to the scene map.

![](img/GettingStarted/013/003.png=600-)

### Draw to the block

Draw to the block, then it means that the block cannot be walked over.

![](img/GettingStarted/013/004.png)

### Test

![](img/GettingStarted/013/005.gif=600-)

### Clear the drawn obstacles

Hold down the shortcut key **shift+left mouse button** to clear it.

![](img/GettingStarted/013/006.gif=600-)
